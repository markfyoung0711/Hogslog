## DeerLab

### Photo Recognition
- Auto-tag bucks, does, turkey, hogs, people and more
- Customize auto-tagging so that certain tags are highlighted, where others are ignored
- Set sensitivity of photo recognition
- Disable photo recognition if desired

### Photo Management, Filtering and Tagging
- Drag and drop thousands of photos to upload at once
- Zoom into photos for easy identification
- Filter photos by date ranges, time ranges, camera location, tags, weather conditions, moon phase, barometric pressure, temperature, humidity, wind speed and wind direction
- Mass edit incorrect timestamps
- Filter by untagged photos, hit list bucks, and daylight hours
- Mass delete images you don't want
- Auto-tag and/or manually tag photos
- Add multiple tags per photo
- Sort photos by time taken and uploaded date
- Customize quick filter to view specific tags or animals you want to keep a tab on

### Property & Camera Management
- Make properties as large as desired
- Set custom daylight hours and time zone by property
- Toggle active and inactive camera locations on Google satellite maps
- Group photos and data by property
- Set quick filter tags by property
- Optional longitude and latitude camera entry

### Animal Profiles
- Dashboard overview for individual or groups of animals
- Sighting reports for time of day, camera activity, wind direction, wind speed, moon phases, and temperature
- Organize profiles by Watching, Hit List, or Harvested statuses
- Filter all profile reports by camera locations, date ranges, as well as time ranges
- View historical and zoom into photo log showing what days profiled animal is seen for lifetime of sightings
- Automated sighting calculations so camera settings won't favor one camera location over another
- Customize image for each profile
- Heat map report showing hot spot activity via a satellite map
- Photo gallery for profiled animal or group of animals

### Miscellaneous Benefits
- Accounts can be shared with others
- Optional Metric conversion for our Canadian friends to the north for temperature, wind speed and barometric pressure
- Create unlimited albums to share photos without sensitive data
- DeerLab works with all trail cameras, Macs, PC's, tablets & smart phones
